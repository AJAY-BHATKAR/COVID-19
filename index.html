Covid-19

STAY SAFE, STAY HOME

Protection from COVID-19

Wear A Face Mask

Wash Your Hands

Avoid Close Contact

Major symptoms of COVID-19

  • Fever
  • Cough
  • Loss of smell
  • difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
  • loss of speech or mobility, or confusion
  • chest pain

Things not to do during Covid:

  • Don't Share Eating
  • Don't Touch Your Face Or Nose
  • Don't Communicate With Sick People

Things to do during Covid:

  • Use sanitizer
  • Wear mask
  • Medical check up

How do I wash HANDS properly?

1

Apply Soap To Your Hands

2

Wash Between The Fingers

3

Wash on tip of the Fingers

4

Wash The Back Of The Hands

5

Clean With Water

6

Wipe out all water from your hands


People Can Catch COVID-19 From Others With The Virus. The Disease Can Spread From Person To Person Through Small Droplets Or Or Mouth That Spread When A Person With COVID-19 Coughs Or Exhales. These Droplets Fall On Objects And Surfaces Around The Person. They Catch COVID-19 Corona Virus As A Result Of Other Uninfected People Then Touching These Objects Or Surfaces And Touching Their Eyes, Nose Or Mouth. Uninfected People Can Also Catch COVID-19 If They Breathe In Droplets From A Person Who Coughs Or Exhales Covid-19 Corona Virus Droplets. Therefore, It Is Very Important To Stay At Least 1 Meter (3 Feet) Away From A Sick Person And Avoid Contact.
